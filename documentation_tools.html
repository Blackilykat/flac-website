--- layout: default title: Tools --- FLAC is a general purpose audio format supported by many programs, but in this section we are concentrating on just the official tools provided by the FLAC project:
  • flac - The command-line encoder and decoder.
  • metaflac - The command-line metadata editor.
Other resources:
  • Bugs - How to report bugs and request features, and a list of known bugs in the FLAC tools.
  • FLAC Mailing List - General discussion about and support for FLAC, tools, releases, etc. (You must subscribe to post.)
  • Hydrogenaudio - General discussion about FLAC and other audio codecs

See Getting FLAC for instructions on downloading and installing the official FLAC tools, or Using FLAC for instructions and guides on playing FLAC files, ripping CDs to FLAC, etc.

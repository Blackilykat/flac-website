--- layout: post title: Several new devices support FLAC --- Another handful of devices that support FLAC are out or announced: the Zensonic Z500 Networked DVD Media Player, Escient's new FireBall E2-40, E2-160, and DVDM-300, the M300A Digital Music Player from Digital Techniques, Meda Systems' Bravo servers, and the MS300 Music Server by McIntosh Laboratory.

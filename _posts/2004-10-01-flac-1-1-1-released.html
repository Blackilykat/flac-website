--- layout: post title: FLAC 1.1.1 released --- There is a new changelog with a complete list of changes/fixes/improvements, but some of the main ones include: Better Ogg FLAC support including seeking and an official Ogg FLAC mapping, significant decoding speedup (almost 2x) on PowerPC (includes Macs with G4/G5), speedups in the plugins, several new options to flac and metaflac by popular demand, several API additions requested by developers and many bugfixes.
